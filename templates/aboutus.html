Department of EEE



About Department

The Department of Electrical Engineering, established in 1946, offers B.Tech and M.Tech programs in various specializations, including Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electrical Power Systems, Power and Industrial Drives, and Control Systems. The department also provides part-time M.Tech programs and Ph.D. research opportunities. The department is equipped with well-furnished laboratories and a library, offering students access to computing facilities, software packages, and learning resources. Faculty members actively engage in research in areas like Electrical Power Systems, Power Electronics, Industrial Drives, Reliability Engineering, and Control Systems. The department has a strong industry connection, with alumni holding significant positions in both public and private sectors. It also collaborates with organizations like PRDC, Bangalore, to foster innovation and development.



Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur



About Institution

JNTUA College of Engineering, Ananthapuramu stands aloft on a solid foundation of past glory and prestige. This college was started in the year 1946, under the post-war reconstruction program and functioned at Guindy campus, Madras for two years before moving to Anantapur in 1948. By 1958, the college settled into the present permanent buildings, including the Administrative block, Laboratories, Engineering Departments, Library, and Hostel blocks. Over the years, the college has been affiliated with Madras University, S V University, and JNTU Hyderabad. Since 2008, it has been a constituent college of JNT University Anantapur. With a campus sprawling over 185 acres, it serves as a center for technical education in a peaceful, conducive environment for learning.
